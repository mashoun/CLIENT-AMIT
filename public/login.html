About us

Who we are

Founded in 2020 by a group of young entrepreneurs, is a global holding company with a vast portfolio of subsidiaries operating across several industries ranging from printing, packaging, and tobacco essentials with high-level expertise in rolling papers. Now, with over X projects completed, Amit remains dedicated to providing its clients with impeccable quality and exceptional services at competitive rates, with the highest standards of integrity and transparency.
